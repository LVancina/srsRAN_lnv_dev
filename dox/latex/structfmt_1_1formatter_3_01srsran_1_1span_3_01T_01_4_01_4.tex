Custom formatter for {\ttfamily span$<$\+T$>$}. 

By default, the elements within the span are separated by a space character. A comma delimiter is available and can be selected by formatting with {\ttfamily \{\+:,\}}. The delimiter can be disabled by formatting with {\ttfamily \{\+:\#\}}.
