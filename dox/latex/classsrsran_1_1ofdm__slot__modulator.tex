\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Describes an OFDM modulator with slot granularity. 

\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
Performs OFDM modulation as per TS 38.\+211 section 5.\+3.\+1 OFDM baseband signal generation for all channels except PRACH. 

In addition to modulation, it applies phase compensation as per TS 38.\+211 section 5.\+4 Modulation and upconversion. 
\end{DoxyRemark}
