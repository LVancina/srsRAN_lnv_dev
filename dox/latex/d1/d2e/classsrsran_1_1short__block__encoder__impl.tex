Encodes and rate-\/matches a small number (less than 12) bits. 

Encodes a short sequence of bits (length 1 to 11, both included), following the specifications of TS38.\+212 Section 5.\+3.\+3, and rate-\/matches the encoded bits according to TS38.\+212 Section 5.\+4.\+3. 
\begin{DoxyParams}[1]{Parameters}
\mbox{\texttt{ out}}  & {\em output} & Sequence of encoded and rate-\/matched bits (i.\+e., $f_0, f_1, \dots, f_{E-1}$ in TS38.\+212). \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ in}}  & {\em input} & Sequence of bits to encode (i.\+e., $c_0, c_1, \dots, c_{K-1}$ in TS38.\+212). \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ in}}  & {\em mod} & Modulation scheme. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
Both {\ttfamily output} and {\ttfamily input} follow an unpacked representation, that is one bit per entry. 

The size $E$ of {\ttfamily output} should be as follows\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $E \ge Q_m$, where $Q_m$ is the modulation order, if {\ttfamily input} is a single bit;
\item $E \ge 3Q_m$ if {\ttfamily input} consists of 2 bits;
\item $E \ge 32$ otherwise ({\ttfamily input} size from 3 to 11 bits). 
\end{DoxyItemize}
\end{DoxyRemark}
