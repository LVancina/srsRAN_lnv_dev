\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Short-\/block encoder unit test. 

The test encodes messages of different lengths (from 1 to 11 bits) into codeblocks (whose length may depend on the modulation scheme). Messages and expected codeblocks (for comparison) are provided by test vectors.
