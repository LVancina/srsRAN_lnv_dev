\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Structure that holds all the information related with the configuration of a UE in the scheduler.

The information held by this structure includes configured cells and logical channels by the upper layers based on the UE RRC configuration, and other RRM-\/related parameters that can be controlled by the app or via O1/\+E2.
