\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
asn.\+1 utilities. 

In this file, we implement several algorithms used to add/mod/release asn.\+1 objects in lists based on their ids. This operations are commonly used during the application of Diffs/\+Deltas when the UE has to be reconfigured.
