\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class that manages the creation/reconfiguration/deletion of UEs from the MAC UL Note\+: The access to this class is thread-\/safe as long as the same UE index and RNTI \% MAX\+\_\+\+NOF\+\_\+\+UES is not accessed from different threads. This is in general assured by the DU manager.
