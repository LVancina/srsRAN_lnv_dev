class srsran\+::static\+\_\+vector$<$ T, MAX\+\_\+\+N $>$}
Random access, variable-\/sized container with embedded storage. Contrarily to std\+::vector$<$\+T$>$, its maximum size is limited at compile-\/time, and it does not rely on allocators. Contrarily to std\+::array, the elements are constructed and destructed dynamically when the container size() changes.
