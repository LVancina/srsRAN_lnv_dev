\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
PHY--FAPI bidirectional adaptor interface. 

This adaptor is a collection of interfaces to translate FAPI messages into their PHY layer counterpart and vice versa.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
All implementations of this public interface must hold the ownership of all its internal components. 
\end{DoxyNote}
