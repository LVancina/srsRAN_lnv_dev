Number of separate PUCCH resource sets for HARQ-\/\+ACK reporting that are available in a cell. 

\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
UEs will be distributed possibly over different HARQ-\/\+ACK PUCCH sets; the more sets, the fewer UEs will have to share the same set, which reduces the chances that UEs won\textquotesingle{}t be allocated PUCCH due to lack of resources. However, the usage of PUCCH-\/dedicated REs will be proportional to the number of sets. 
\end{DoxyRemark}
