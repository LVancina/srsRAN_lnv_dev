\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Sampling rate. 

This class abstracts the concept of sampling rate from its units of measure. Specifically, the sampling rate is stored, privately, in a convenient representation and it can be accessed by getter and setter methods that automatically convert the sampling rate to and from, respectively, the desired representation/unit of measure.
