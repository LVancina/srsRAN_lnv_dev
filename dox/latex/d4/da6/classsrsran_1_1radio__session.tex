Stops the radio session operation. 

Any call to radio operation after calling \doxylink{classsrsran_1_1radio__session_aeb7baacf9aa88b71e9968d8e46d970c2}{stop()} will return instantly without interacting with the physical radio.

\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
Calling \doxylink{classsrsran_1_1radio__session_aeb7baacf9aa88b71e9968d8e46d970c2}{stop()} without having called \doxylink{classsrsran_1_1radio__session_a793081cb27d4a66be880959af97fa9ea}{start()} results in undefined behavior. 

Calling \doxylink{classsrsran_1_1radio__session_a793081cb27d4a66be880959af97fa9ea}{start()} after \doxylink{classsrsran_1_1radio__session_aeb7baacf9aa88b71e9968d8e46d970c2}{stop()} results in undefined behavior.
