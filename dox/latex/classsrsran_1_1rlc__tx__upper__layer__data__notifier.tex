\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This interface represents the data upper layer that the TX RLC bearer must notify on transmission and/or delivery of SDUs it can stop its discard timer.

The following events shall be notified\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item on transmission of an SDU, i.\+e. pop from SDU queue
\item on successful delivery of an SDU (only RLC AM) 
\end{DoxyItemize}
