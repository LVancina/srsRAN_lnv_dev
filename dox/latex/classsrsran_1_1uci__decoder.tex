\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Uplink Control Information decoder. 

Decodes UCI payloads containing SR, HARQ-\/\+ACK and/or CSI bits, transmitted on PUCCH, reversing the steps described in TS38.\+212 Sections 6.\+3.\+1.\+2, 6.\+3.\+1.\+3, 6.\+3.\+1.\+4 and 6.\+3.\+1.\+5.

Decodes UCI payloads of either HARQ-\/\+ACK or CSI bits, transmitted on PUSCH, reversing the steps described in TS38.\+212 Sections 6.\+3.\+2.\+2, 6.\+3.\+2.\+3 and 6.\+3.\+2.\+4.
