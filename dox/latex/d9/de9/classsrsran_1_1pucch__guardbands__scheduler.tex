\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This class implements a scheduler that pre-\/reserve the PUCCH resources\textquotesingle{} REs on the scheduler grid, to prevent the PUSCH allocator from using them. The pre-\/reserved REs are hereby referred as PUCCH guardbands.

Runs the PUCCH guardbands scheduler. 

When called for the first time, this function pre-\/reserves the PUCCH\textquotesingle{}s REs over the entire grid, until the last (farthest in the future) usable slot. From the second time onwards, the scheduler pre-\/reserves the PUCCH for the last usable slot of the grid.
