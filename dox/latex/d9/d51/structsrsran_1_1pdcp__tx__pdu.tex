\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Structure used to represent a PDCP PDU in Tx direction. Depending on the bearer type (SRB/\+DRB) and PDU type (control/data) the PDU is accompanied with the associated pdcp\+\_\+sn. For data PDUs on DRBs, pdcp\+\_\+sn shall be set to the PDCP sequence number (SN) of the PDU. For control PDUs on DRBs, pdcp\+\_\+sn shall be unset and ignored. For any PDU on SRBs, pdcp\+\_\+sn shall be unset and ignored.
