\textbackslash{}brif DM-\/\+RS amplitude scaling factor.

Parameter $\beta _{\textup{PUSCH}}^{\textup{DMRS}}$ as per TS38.\+211 Section 6.\+4.\+1.\+1.\+3. It must be set to $\beta _{\textup{PUSCH}}^{\textup{DMRS}}=10^{-\beta_{\textup{DMRS}}/20}$, as per TS38.\+214 Section 6.\+2.\+2, where $\beta_{\textup{DMRS}}$ is the PUSCH EPRE to DM-\/\+RS EPRE ratio expressed in decibels, as specified in TS38.\+214 Table 6.\+2.\+2-\/1.
