\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This interface represents the RLC TX AM entity that the RLC RX AM must notify to inform the RLC TX AM entity that a status report is triggered (either by timeout or by request of the other peer) or its size has changed (e.\+g. further PDUs have been received)
