bool bit_encoder::pack ( uint64_t val,
uint32_t n_bits )

Append provided bitmap into byte_buffer held by bit_encoder. The bitmap to pack is left aligned (i.e. MSB of bitmap will be the last bit to be packed in underlying buffer). Example:

  • Before: [...][11______]
  • pack(0b1011, 4): [...][111011__]
  • pack(0b01100, 5): [...][11101101][100_____]
    Parameters
