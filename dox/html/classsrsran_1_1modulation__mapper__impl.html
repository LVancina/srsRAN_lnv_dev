Maps a sequence of bits to a sequence of floating point complex symbols according to the given modulation scheme.

Parameters
[in]inputThe sequence of bits. Each entry corresponds to a single bit.
[out]symbolsThe resulting sequence of complex-valued symbols.
[in]schemeThe modulation scheme determining the mapping.
Remarks
The length of input should be equal to the length of symbols times the modulation order (that is, the number of bits per modulated symbol).
