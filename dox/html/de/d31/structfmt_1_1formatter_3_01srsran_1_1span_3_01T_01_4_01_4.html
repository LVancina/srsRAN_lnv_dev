Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct fmt::formatter< srsran::span< T > >

Custom formatter for span<T>.

By default, the elements within the span are separated by a space character. A comma delimiter is available and can be selected by formatting with {:,}. The delimiter can be disabled by formatting with {:#}.
