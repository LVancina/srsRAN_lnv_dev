◆ bch_payload

std::array<uint8_t, BCH_PAYLOAD_SIZE> srsran::ssb_processor::pdu_t::bch_payload

PBCH payload. The first 24 bits correspond to the packed MIB, while the last 8 bits are:

  • SFN (4 bit),
  • half-radio frame (1 bit), and
  • SS/PBCH block index (3 bit) for L_max=64, otherwise
  • SS/PBCH subcarrier offset (1 bit) and 2 padding bits.
    Remarks
    Bit order is specified in 3GPP TS38.212 Section 7.1.1.
