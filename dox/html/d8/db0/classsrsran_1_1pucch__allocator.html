Allocate the common PUCCH resource for HARQ-ACK for a given UE.

Parameters
[in,out]slot_allocstruct with scheduling results.
[in]tcrntitemporary RNTI of the UE.
[in]k0k0 value, or delay (in slots) of PDSCH slot vs the corresponding PDCCH slot.
[in]k1delay in slots of the UE's PUCCH HARQ-ACK report with respect to the PDSCH.
[in]dci_infoinformation with DL DCI, needed for HARQ-(N)-ACK scheduling info.
Returns
The PUCCH resource indicator, if the allocation is successful; a nullopt otherwise.
Remarks
pucch_res_indicator, or , is the PUCCH resource indicator< > field for DCI 1_0 and 1_1 as per TS 38.213, Section 9.2.1. It indicates the UE which PUCCH resource should be used for HACK-(N)ACK reporting.
