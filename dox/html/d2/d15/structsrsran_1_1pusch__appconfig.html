◆ dc_offset

dc_offset_t srsran::pusch_appconfig::dc_offset = dc_offset_t::center

Direct Current (DC) offset, in number of subcarriers, used in PUSCH.

The numerology of the active UL BWP is used as a reference to determine the number of subcarriers. The DC offset value 0 corresponds to the center of the SCS-Carrier for the numerology of the active UL BWP.
