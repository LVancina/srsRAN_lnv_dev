Detailed Description

Decodes MAC Rx PDUs and handles the respective CEs and SDUs by dispatching them to the Scheduler or UE UL bearers. This class is thread-safe, i.e. it can be concurrently called for multiple MAC UL PDUs, iff its methods are called from within the respective MAC PDU RNTI executor.
