uint16_t srsran::ldpc_encoder_impl::codeblock_length = 52
protected

Number of encoded bits needed to fill the output vector.

Instead of computing all the variable nodes, the encoder can work with just enough bits to fill the vector returned by the ldpc_encoder_impl::encode function. It is a multiple of the lifting size.
