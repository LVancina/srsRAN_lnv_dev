Detailed Description

Recycling bin for discarded PDUs that shall be deleted by a different executor off a real-time critical path. This class is intended to offload the time-consumping deletion of thousands byte_buffer objects of ACK'ed PDUs from tx_window upon reception of the RLC AM status report, which is handled by the pcell_executor in a real-time critical path.
