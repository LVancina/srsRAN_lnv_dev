Parameter as per TS38.211 Section 6.4.1.1.3. It must be set to , as per TS38.214 Section 6.2.2, where is the PUSCH EPRE to DM-RS EPRE ratio expressed in decibels, as specified in TS38.214 Table 6.2.2-1.
