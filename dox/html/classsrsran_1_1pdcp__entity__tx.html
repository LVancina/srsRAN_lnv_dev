Base class used for transmitting PDCP bearers. It provides interfaces for the PDCP bearers, for the higher and lower layers

◆ handle_delivery_notification()

void pdcp_entity_tx::handle_delivery_notification ( uint32_t highest_sn)
overridevirtual

Informs the PDCP about the highest PDCP PDU sequence number of the PDCP PDU that was successfully delivered in sequence towards the UE.

In case of RLC AM, the PDCP will stop the discard timers for all PDUs up to highest_sn. In case of RLC UM, the PDCP will ignore this notification.

Parameters
highest_snHighest in a sequence delivered PDCP PDU sequence number.

Implements srsran::pdcp_tx_lower_interface.

Informs the PDCP entity about the highest PDCP PDU sequence number of the PDCP PDU that was transmitted by the lower layers (i.e. by the RLC).

In case of RLC AM, the PDCP will ignore this notification. In case of RLC UM, the PDCP will stop the discard timers for all PDUs up to highest_sn.
