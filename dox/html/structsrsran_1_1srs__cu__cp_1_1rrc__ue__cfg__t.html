unsigned srsran::srs_cu_cp::rrc_ue_cfg_t::rrc_procedure_timeout_ms

Timeout used for RRC message exchange with UE. It needs to suit the expected communication delay and account for potential retransmissions (HARQ and RLC), UE processing delays (see Sec 12 in TS 38.331), SR delays, etc.
