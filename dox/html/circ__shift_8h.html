Circularly shifts a sequence in the backward direction.

The element at position i is moved to position i - shift<\tt>. Elements that fall beyond the beginning of the sequence are reintroduced at its end.

Parameters
[out]outShifted output sequence.
[in]inOriginal input sequence.
[in]shiftThe number of positions the sequence is shifted by.
Remarks
Cannot be used to override memory.
