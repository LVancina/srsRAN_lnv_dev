Soft demodulation.

Demodulates complex symbols into log-likelihood ratios (i.e., soft bits).

Parameters
[out]llrsDemodulated log-likelihood ratios.
[in]symbolsComplex symbols to demodulate (equalization output).
[in]noise_varsNoise variances (after equalization).
[in]modModulation scheme.
Remarks
Parameters symbols and noise_vars must have the same length. Each entry of noise_vars contains the (estimated) noise variance after equalization corresponding to the symbol with the same index.
The length of llrs is given by the number of symbols multiplied by the modulation order of mod.
Any operation involving ill-formed inputs (NaN, infinity or negative noise variances, as well as NaN-valued symbols or infinity-over-infinity divisions) will result in an indeterminate, zero-valued log- likelihood ratio.
