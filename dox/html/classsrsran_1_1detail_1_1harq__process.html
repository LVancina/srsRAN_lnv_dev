◆ last_slot_ack

template<bool IsDownlink>
slot_point srsran::detail::harq_process< IsDownlink >::last_slot_ack
protected

For DL, slot_ack is the slot at which gNB is expected to receive the ACK via UCI. For UL, slot_ack is the slot when the PUSCH CRC will be received by the gNB. It coincides with slot_tx of UL.

◆ last_slot_tx

template<bool IsDownlink>
slot_point srsran::detail::harq_process< IsDownlink >::last_slot_tx
protected

For DL, slot_tx corresponds to the slot when the TB in the HARQ process is going to be transmitted by the gNB. For UL, slot_tx corresponds to the slot when the TB in the HARQ process is going to be transmitted by the UE.
