Equalizes the MIMO channel and combines Tx–Rx paths.

For each transmit layer, the contributions of all receive ports are combined with weights obtained from the estimated channel coefficients. The variance of the point-to-point equivalent noise perturbing each modulated symbol is also estimated.

Parameters
[out]eq_symbolsEqualized modulation symbols, organized by transmission layer.
[out]eq_noise_varsPost-equalization noise variances, organized by transmission layer.
[in]ch_symbolsChannel symbols, i.e., complex samples organized by receive port.
[in]ch_estimatesChannel estimation coefficients, indexed by receive port and transmission layer.
[in]noise_var_estimatesNoise variance estimation for each receive port.
[in]tx_scalingTransmission gain scaling factor.
Note
The sizes of eq_symbols, eq_noise_vars, ch_symbols and noise_var_estimates must be consistent with the ch_estimates channel dimensions.
Warning
If, for a given transmitted RE, the combined channel across all paths results in a non-normal value (zero, infinity or NaN), the corresponding equalized modulation RE and equalized noise variance will be set to zero and infinity, respectively.
If the noise_var_estimates noise variances have ill-formed values (zero, negative, infinity or NaN), the corresponding equalized modulation REs and equalized noise variances will be set to zero and infinity, respectively.
