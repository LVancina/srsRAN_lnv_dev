Detailed Description

This interface represents the data entry point of the transmitting side of a F1-U bearer of the CU-UP. The upper layer (e.g. PDCP) will push NR-U SDUs (e.g. PDCP PDUs/RLC SDUs) into the F1-U bearer towards the DU. The upper layer (e.g. PDCP) will also inform the lower layer of SDUs (e.g. PDCP PDUs) to be discarded.

Enqueues a notification to discard the given PDCP TX PDU at the DU.

The queued notifications are either piggy-backed with the next SDU or sent periodically (whatever comes first).

Parameters
