virtual async_task< void > srsran::srs_du::du_ue_controller::disconnect_notifiers ( )
pure virtual

Disconnect the UE inter-layer notifiers.

This method should be called as a first step in the deletion of a UE, to ensure traffic is not flowing through its bearers during the layer by layer UE context removal.
