void f1u_bearer_impl::handle_delivery_notification ( uint32_t highest_pdcp_sn)
overridevirtual

Handles a delivery notification from lower layers (i.e. from RLC AM).

Notification must be performed in ascending order of PDCP sequence numbers. It is the responsibility of the calling function (i.e. the RLC) to prevent out-of-order notifications.

This function is quick and shall be called directly from pcell_executor to avoid excessive transitions across executors.

Safe execution from: pcell_executor
