task_executor* srsran::worker_manager::cu_cp_exec = nullptr

du ctrl exec points to general ctrl_worker du ue exec points to the general ue_worker cu-cp ctrl exec points to general ctrl_worker (just like the du ctrl exec) cu-up ue exec points to the general ue_worker (just like the du ue exec)

The handler side is responsible for executor dispatching:

  • ngap::handle_message calls cu-cp ctrl exec
  • f1ap_cu::handle_message calls cu-cp ctrl exec
  • e1ap_cu_cp::handle_message calls cu-cp ctrl exec
  • e1ap_cu_up::handle_message calls cu-up ue exec
