optional<unsigned> srsran::csi_report_config::report_frequency_config::nof_csi_reporting_subbands

Indicates a contiguous or non-contiguous subset of subbands in the bandwidth part which CSI shall be reported for. This field is absent if there are less than 24 PRBs. Values {3,...,19}. See TS 38.331, csi-ReportingBand.
